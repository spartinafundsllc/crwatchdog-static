## Why Consumer Reports’ mattress picks matter (and what they actually test)

Most “best mattress” lists online are built around affiliate commissions. CR’s list is different because it’s built around measurable performance.

Here’s what matters in testing—because it’s what matters at 2 a.m.:

- **Support (spinal alignment):** Does the bed keep your spine aligned for back and side sleeping, across different body sizes?
- **Firmness:** How soft or firm the mattress *actually* is on a standardized scale (not the brand’s made-up label).
- **Stability / motion isolation:** How much movement transfers when a partner shifts, rolls over, or gets up.
- **Durability:** A long-run stress test meant to simulate years of use and check for sagging or performance drop-off.
- **Owner experience:** Comfort and satisfaction feedback from a very large pool of real owners.

The result is a list that tends to reward mattresses that are boring in the best way: supportive, consistent, and less likely to become a regret purchase.
