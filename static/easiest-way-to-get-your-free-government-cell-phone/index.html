Here’s the Easiest Way to Get Your Free Government Cell Phone

You are here: Home / Best Tech / Here’s the Easiest Way to Get Your Free Government Cell Phone

March 22, 2021 By Paolo Roxas
Categories: Best Tech Tags: aid, Free Government Phone, lifeline, lifeline assistance program, Lifeline Program

The US government provides a free phone program called Lifeline Assistance.  It helps low-income citizens get access to free mobile phones with affordable plans. It is available in all states, including the District of Columbia and Puerto Rico to assist you in getting in touch with work opportunities or your loved ones. You need to qualify, but it is easy if you already are using one of the government’s aid services.  You probably are already entitled to a Lifeline Program cellphone.  Your cellphone will not come from T-Mobile, Verizon, AT&T, or Sprint.  But it will be a high-quality phone that gets the job done.

Here is how you get a free cellphone from the government:

  1. Prove that you qualify.
  2. Visit the website of the company for your state.
  3. Submit the documents online.
  4. That’s it!

How to Qualify for a Free Government Cell Phone

The FCC provides strict guidelines in allowing individuals to qualify for the Lifeline Assistance aid. But its easy since you are automatically eligible for a free cell phone and an affordable plan if you have the following aid programs:

  • Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Medicaid
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance
  • Tribal-specific programs: Bureau of Indian Affairs General Assistance, Tribally-Administered Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TTANF), Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations (FDPIR), Head Start

You will also need to submit pay stubs, a W-2 form, or a letter from the government agency that you belong to. Most providers have an option where you can upload a scanned file of these documents so you won’t need to mail them.

You can also use this website (click the link) to easily know if you are eligible along with some instructions to get a free Government cell phone.

Still confused? Each company has various ways to get you qualified, so we suggest reading below. Choose a company that lists your state, and give them a call, send an email, or explore their website.
