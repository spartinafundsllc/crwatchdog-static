Favorite Finds of 2025: Products We Love

You are here: Home / Uncategorized / Favorite Finds of 2025: Products We Love

January 10, 2025 By Paolo Roxas
Categories: Uncategorized Tags: consumer reports, Home Appliances

At CRwatchdog, we’re dedicated to helping you make informed purchasing decisions. We don’t just test products in labs; we use them in our everyday lives. This year, we’ve compiled a list of some of our absolute favorites – the items we find ourselves reaching for again and again. These aren’t necessarily the highest-rated in our rigorous testing, but they’ve earned a special place in our hearts (and homes) due to their unique features, practicality, and overall user experience.  This list is derived from Consumer Reports top staff picks.

Kitchen Must-Haves

These kitchen must-haves aren’t merely just fancy purchases since they actually upgrade the functionality of it. From smaller items like pans to brilliant gas ranges, these will make you feel like a true pro chef in no time.

Ninja Foodi Digital Air Fryer Oven (DT401)

Buy Now from Amazon

This isn’t your average toaster oven; it’s a kitchen powerhouse. Beyond simply toasting bread, it offers a range of cooking functions, including air frying, roasting, baking, broiling, and even dehydrating. The spacious interior allows you to cook larger meals, and the dual-zone technology on some models allows you to cook two different foods at two different temperatures simultaneously. This can be a game-changer for weeknight dinners, allowing you to roast vegetables in one zone while air-frying chicken or fish in the other. This saves not only time but also valuable counter space by consolidating multiple appliances into one. Look for models with precise temperature controls, pre-programmed settings, and easy-to-clean interiors.

Cuisinart PerfecTemp Cordless Electric Kettle (CPK-17)

Buy Now from Amazon

For the discerning tea drinker, temperature matters. Different types of tea require specific water temperatures to extract their full flavor profile. The Cuisinart PerfecTemp takes the guesswork out of brewing with pre-set temperature controls for various teas, including green (175°F), white (185°F), oolong (190°F), and black (212°F). It also has a “Keep Warm” option that maintains the selected temperature for 30 minutes. Features like a cordless design, a 360° swivel base, and automatic shut-off add convenience and safety.

Frigidaire Gallery GCFI3060BF Gas Range

Buy Now from Amazon

This gas range offers superior cooking performance compared to many electric ranges. It provides instant heat and precise temperature control, making it ideal for searing, sautéing, and simmering. The powerful burners deliver consistent heat distribution for even cooking, while features like a simmer burner allow for gentle simmering without scorching. The oven often includes convection baking and air frying capabilities, expanding your cooking options. Look for sealed burners for easy cleanup and continuous grates for sliding pots and pans seamlessly across the cooktop.

Oxo Ceramic Professional Non-Stick Pan

Buy Now from Amazon

Concerns about the safety of traditional non-stick coatings are valid. The Oxo Ceramic Professional Non-Stick Pan addresses these concerns with its PFOA-free ceramic non-stick coating, which is free of harmful chemicals like PFAS, lead, and cadmium. The durable construction, featuring hard-anodized aluminum, ensures even heat distribution and prevents warping. The pan is also oven-safe up to 390°F. The smooth non-stick surface makes cooking and cleaning a breeze.

Anyday Collection Cookware

Buy Now from Amazon

This innovative cookware set is designed for modern living. The pieces are designed to be used for cooking, serving, and storing food, minimizing the need for multiple dishes and containers. The unique bowl shapes are perfect for enjoying meals directly from the cookware, and the steam-trapping lids help retain moisture and promote even cooking in the microwave. These sets are often microwave and dishwasher safe, adding to their convenience.

Tech That Enhances Everyday Life

Too much tech can be good or bad, but these will make your daily living fantastic. These will enhance both relaxation and productivity, so they are great upgrades for 2025.

Soundcore Sleep A20 Bluetooth Headphones

Buy Now from Amazon

These headphones are designed to help you fall asleep and stay asleep by masking unwanted noise with soothing sounds or white noise. Unlike traditional noise-canceling headphones, they prioritize comfort for sleeping, with small, lightweight earbuds that fit snugly in the ear. Look for models with a comfortable fit, long battery life (up to 14 hours on a single charge), and a variety of sound options. The Soundcore app offers a library of white noise, nature sounds, and sleep stories.

Sony WF-1000XM5 Earbuds

Buy Now from Amazon

These earbuds provide an immersive audio experience with industry-leading noise cancellation. They’re perfect for listening to music, podcasts, or audiobooks in noisy environments. Look for features like active noise cancellation (ANC), transparency mode (to hear your surroundings when needed), and comfortable ear tips for extended wear. The WF-1000XM5s also boast improved call quality and multipoint connection, allowing you to connect to two devices simultaneously.

HP Envy 14-FA0023DX Laptop

Buy Now from Amazon

This laptop prioritizes ease of use with features designed to make computing more accessible. Large keys, a responsive touchscreen, and facial recognition login simplify navigation and access. The HP Envy 14 also features a powerful Intel Core processor, ample RAM, and a fast SSD for smooth performance.

Edifier S1000MKII Bookshelf Speakers

Buy Now from Amazon

These speakers deliver exceptional sound quality for music lovers. They are designed to be placed on shelves or stands and provide a wider soundstage than smaller portable speakers. The Edifier S1000MKIIs feature Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity, optical and coaxial inputs, and a powerful amplifier for rich, detailed sound. They’re a great choice for both vinyl enthusiasts and digital music streamers.

Around the House and Beyond

Improving your household and its surroundings is also a valid goal for this year. These tools will not only make life easier, but also make each task pleasurable and less time consuming.

Atlas 80v Brushless 18″ Chainsaw: This battery-powered chainsaw offers the convenience of cordless operation without sacrificing power. It’s ideal for homeowners who need to trim branches, cut firewood, or clear small trees. Look for features like a brushless motor for increased efficiency and longer runtime, an automatic chain oiler, and a tool-less chain tensioning system for easy maintenance.

Coop Sleep Goods The Original Pillow

Buy Now from Amazon

This adjustable pillow allows you to personalize the level of support by adding or removing fill. This is especially beneficial for combination sleepers who change positions throughout the night. The Coop Sleep Goods pillow features a blend of shredded memory foam and microfiber fill and comes with extra fill so you can customize it to your liking.

Black+Decker BDH2000L Handheld Vacuum

Buy Now from Amazon

This lightweight and portable vacuum is perfect for quick cleanups around the house. Its cordless design allows for easy maneuverability, and attachments like a crevice tool and flip-up brush make it versatile for cleaning various surfaces. The BDH2000L also features a cyclonic action that helps keep the filter clean and maintain strong suction.

ChomChom Roller Pet Hair Remover

Buy Now from Amazon

This reusable tool effectively removes pet hair from furniture, clothing, and other surfaces without the need for sticky refills. It’s a waste-free and eco-friendly alternative to traditional lint rollers. The ChomChom Roller uses a patented brush system to trap and lift pet hair, which is then collected in a built-in chamber.

Nuna TRVL LX Stroller

Buy Now from Amazon

This stroller offers a great balance of portability and durability. It’s lightweight and easy to fold, making it convenient for travel and storage. The Nuna TRVL LX also features a comfortable seat with multiple recline positions, an adjustable handlebar, and a large storage basket.

Tommy Bahama Wavy Marlin Deluxe Backpack Beach Chair

Buy Now from Amazon

This beach chair is designed for ultimate relaxation and convenience. It features multiple reclining positions, built-in storage compartments for snacks and drinks, and even a cooler. The Tommy Bahama chair is also lightweight and easy to carry with its padded backpack straps.

Dagne Dover Landon Carryall

Buy Now from Amazon

This durable and stylish bag is perfect for travel, work, or everyday use. Its spacious interior and multiple compartments provide ample storage for all your essentials. The Dagne Dover Landon Carryall is made from water-resistant neoprene and features a padded laptop compartment and a removable shoulder strap.
